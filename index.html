Boring and Bulky

Having one or even multiple physical cookbooks, or index cards, filled with your Family's passed down secrets can be cumbersome.

Even worse if you say lose one part, or it becomes damaged.

Our Modern approach to allow you to put those heirlooms to rest where they'll be protected with our application:

Secret Family Recipe Cookbook.

How does it work?

That's the Easy part.

How easy you ask? It's as simple as Upload, Type in, and Submit.

First you Upload a picture, or multiple pictures.

Then you type in your recipe, including: ingredients, measurements, and times.

After that, you submit your recipe, and you'll be taken to your own personal
dashboard to view them and select which one you want to use.
